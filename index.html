Frequently Asked Questions

+

How much does a project cost?

Project costs vary based on the services you need. Event coverage depends on duration and complexity, app development on features, and marketing on campaign scope. Contact us for a detailed, custom quote based on your specific requirements.

+

What is the timeline for a project?

A simple event recording can be turned around in a few days. A custom app development project can take several weeks to months. We work with you to establish a clear timeline upfront, and we pride ourselves on meeting our deadlines.

+

Can you just handle one part, like only the live stream?

Absolutely! While we excel at integrated projects, we are happy to provide any of our services à la carte. Whether you only need event videography, app development, or a digital marketing campaign, we can tailor a solution for you.

+

Do I own the final product, like the app and video files?

Yes. Upon final payment, you own the rights to all final deliverables. For video, this includes the edited files. For app development, you own the source code and the final application. We believe in empowering our clients.

+

How do we get started?

It starts with a conversation. Contact us with your idea, and we'll schedule a call to discuss your goals. We'll then provide a detailed proposal and quote. Once approved, we move into the planning and execution phase for your project.
